Why SmartAI

From system navigation to instant business access.

In many insurance systems, users must navigate through different modules to find policy details, quote status, claim information, payment records, document evidence, or management reports. SmartAI brings these activities into one intelligent workspace.

Users can ask SmartAI what they need, and the platform presents relevant business information, operational insights, document summaries, predictive analysis, and workflow actions in a clear business format.

Core capabilities

What SmartAI does for insurance and business teams.

SmartAI combines ITL's AI models, enterprise workflow automation, business intelligence, and document analysis to support daily insurance operations from one workspace.

Answers General Questions

Users can ask about policies, quotes, claims, payments, operations, and business performance in natural language.

Provides Business Insights

Management can view premium booked, issued policies, pending quotes, pending claims, value at risk, renewals, GWP, and contribution.

Reads Documents

Uploaded policy and claim documents can be read, summarized, and converted into clear review notes using AI-assisted document intelligence.

Checks Policy Forgery Signals

SmartAI can flag suspicious or inconsistent policy documents for human review, with evidence, reference details, and confidence context.

Performs Predictive Analysis

SmartAI supports predictive analysis on claims, policies, and quotes, including quote conversion scoring and operational risk signals.

Supports Insurance Workflows

Create quotes, convert them into policies, capture payments, initiate claims, and upload documents for policies and claims.
